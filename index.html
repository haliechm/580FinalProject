Pong

[two-switch game] Switch and keyboard accessible. Use spacebar & enter key, or directly use switch buttons with mouse or touchscreen. The classic Pong game but the ball freezes before reaching the player's paddle. The player can then toggle between three choices of where to place the paddle using the space bar, and choose with the enter key.

Connect4

[two-switch game] Switch and keyboard accessible. Use spacebar & enter key, or directly use switch buttons with mouse or touchscreen. Play Connect4 either against the computer or a friend. The player can toggle between choices using the space bar and choose a column with the enter key.
